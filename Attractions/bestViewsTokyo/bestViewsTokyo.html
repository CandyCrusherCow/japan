Best Veiws in Tokyo

Written by Babbling Gaijin

There is art in every aspect of Japan but here are some of the best views in Japan.

Kabukicho Tower

Go to jam 17 and enjoy the free Terrace with beautiful views. It was so beautiful I went once in the morning and once at night.(above is the photo in the day, article picture is kabukicho at night)

Shinjuku

Really there's nothing like Shinjuku, it's truly unique and awe inspiring.

Shibuya

Shopping District, gorgeous area to sit and people watch.

Tokyo Imperial Palace

Massive beautiful Japanese Gardens

Golden Gai

Late night spot with alleyways full of bars. Find the best sake and most unique bars in japan.(Bar in photo is at ARAKU)
