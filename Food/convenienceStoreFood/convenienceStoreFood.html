Convenient Snacks; Convenience Store Food I Miss the Most!

Written by Babbling Gaijin

The convenience store is a perfect place to eat breakfast, if you are an early riser or just extremely jet lagged most restaurants are not open until 9 am. It's extremely convenient and has a great selection of items to choose from, especially for people on a budget.

The first thing to know is that not all convenience stores are equal. Meaning they do not sell the same things Seven Eleven and Family Mart has a great selection of savory food and Lawson's is best for sweet desserts.

Here are my absolute favorites to get at the convenience store!

Onigiri

Onigiri is a savory snack that is essential for any trip within Japan. It is seaweed-wrapped rice with fillings like Salmon, Chicken, Pork, and Beef.

Chicken Tail Skewer

A very fatty cut of chicken that is grilled with a teriyaki sauce. Absolutely to die for.

Mochi

Mochi is a gummy pocket with a sweet red bean paste or cream filling. The mochi at the convenience store is top-tier. I highly reccomend the milk cream mochi, it's so good and the mochi rolls at Lawsons.

Dokoyaki

Dokoyaki is a pancake filled with red bean paste and cream. It was one of the first things we tried at Lawsons and it is definitely one of the best!
